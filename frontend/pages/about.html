Why LegalEase AI?
Over 90% of Pakistanis lack higher education. Legal documents written in complex English are a daily barrier that puts millions at risk every time they sign.

Why It Will Work in Pakistan
📋
Contracts Are Written in Complex English
Rental agreements, loan documents, employment contracts. Almost all legal documents in Pakistan are in dense English that average citizens cannot parse. LegalEase AI bridges this gap instantly.
💰
Lawyers Are Too Expensive for Most
A legal consultation costs PKR 5,000 to 50,000. LegalEase AI does not replace lawyers. It empowers ordinary people to understand what they are signing before they are trapped by unfair terms.
🇵🇰
Built for Urdu Speakers
Explanations are in plain, culturally appropriate Urdu using the Noto Nastaliq font. Not just literal translation, but contextual simplification that resonates with everyday Pakistani users.
🛡️
Instant Risk Detection
Termination clauses, penalty traps, liability waivers, arbitration locks. Automatically flagged with Red, Yellow, and Green risk ratings so risks are visible at a glance before signing.
